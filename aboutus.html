Gianatics Developers Limited is a quad group consisting of second year students from FEU TECH BSIT-WMA TW22. Although the logo is fictional, the group also made use of proper graphic design present in the logo. The primary objective of the group is to redesign the current website of the Pokemon Go Web Store, which is the final requirement for passing the Advance Web Design course for the second trimester.

The logo consists of:

  • A black and white tile from a chess board, which symbolizes trickiness; chess is a tricky sport, therefore the project is tricky.
  • The initials "G" and "N" are present to symbolize the word "Gianatics", the chosen group name.
  • The "<>" is placed at the top tile since it symbolizes the HTML entities which the group used all throughout the code. It is above the curly brackets (CSS) to show that HTML is the primary step in making websites.
  • The curly brackets symbolize the proper, appealing, and responsive design of the website by implementing beautiful and marvelous CSS design. Also thanks to Bootstrap.
  • It is surrounded outside by the group name (Gianatics Developers Limited), and the year of development (11111101000 or 2024 in decimal). It is in binary code to make it look cool.
  • Lastly, since the group consists of students, we placed the name of the university.
